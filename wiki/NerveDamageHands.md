# Hand Nerve Damage  
> Leaving a Tourniquet on for too long can cause dangerous nerve damage.<br>Nerve damage will cause pain and add different <b>time penalties</b> depending on the limb that got hurt.<br><br>Nerve damage should slowly heal on it's own as long as you don't have a <b>Tourniquet</b> equipped.
