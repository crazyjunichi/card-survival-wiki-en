# Cooking(Skill)  
> Shows how good you are at cooking. Unlocks new recipes as it gets higher.  
> Cooking is a skill that will grant you new <b>Cooking Recipes</b> as it gets higher.<br><br>It can be increased by preparing <b>meals</b> or by <b>roasting</b> food on a fire.

<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Value Range: </b> 0 ~ 150 </div>  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Base Rate: </b> - </div>
