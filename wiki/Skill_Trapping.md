# Trapping(Skill)  
> Shows how good you are at trapping. Increases your success chances with traps and unlocks new blueprints as it gets higher.  
> "Your trapping skill determines which trap blueprints are at your disposal as well as your <b>Chances</b> of actually catching prey.<br><br>Most traps need <b>Bait</b> to work. Make sure you use the appropriate bait for the <b>Animal</b> you're trying to catch. <br><br>Do not change <b>Bait</b> unless you really think it's necessary  
  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Base Value: </b> 0 </div>  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Value Range: </b> 0 ~ 150 </div>  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Base Rate: </b> - </div>  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Staleness: </b>Staleness multiplier 80% in <font data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="top" title="12TP">3h</font>(stack:2) </div>
