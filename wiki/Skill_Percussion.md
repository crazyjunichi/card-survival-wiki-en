# Percussion(Skill)  
> Shows how good you are at playing percussive instruments. Unlocks new rhythms as it gets higher.  
> "This skill allows you to play <b>Drums</b> and other percussive instruments.<br><br>The higher your skill the more <b>Rhythms</b> you can play.<br>Different Rhythms have different effects and durations. They generally increase your <b>Morale</b>  
  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Base Value: </b> 0 </div>  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Value Range: </b> 0 ~ 150 </div>  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Base Rate: </b> - </div>  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Staleness: </b>Staleness multiplier 80% in <font data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="top" title="12TP">3h</font>(stack:2) </div>
