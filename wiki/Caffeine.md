# Caffeine  
> Keeps you awake but increases your stress and dehydration.  
> Caffeine is a stimulant that will help you stay awake at the cost of some stress and dehydration.<br>It can be ingested from Coffee Berries and any of their derivatives.<br><br>Coffee can be made by roasting Coffee Berry seeds and applying them to boiling water.
