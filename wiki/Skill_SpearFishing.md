# Spear Fishing(Skill)  
> Shows how good you are at fishing with spears and harpoons. Increases your chances of success and the quality of the fish when fishing.  
> "This skill increases your <b>chances</b> of catching fish when uses spears and makes it more likely that you'll catch better <b>types</b>. <br>It also unlocks some blueprints like <b>Fishing Spears and Harpoons</b>.<br><br>While spearfishing is faster than line fishing
