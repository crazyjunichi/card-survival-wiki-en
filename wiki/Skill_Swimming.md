# Swimming(Skill)  
> Shows how good you are at swimming. Increases your chances of reaching difficult places and finding good things when diving   
> Swimming is a skill to allows you to find things while <b>diving</b> and increases your chances of success when doing things in the water.<br><br>This skill lowers your chances of getting hurt by animals underwater or of failing long-distance swimming actions.  
  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Base Value: </b> 0 </div>  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Value Range: </b> 0 ~ 150 </div>  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Base Rate: </b> - </div>  
<div style="font-size:1.2em"><b>Staleness: </b>Staleness multiplier 80% in <font data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="top" title="12TP">3h</font>(stack:2) </div>
