> Reduces the amount of bugs around you (including mosquitoes).  
> This gel can be applied to your body to increase your bug protection and help avoid bug bites.<br><br>It can be made with <b>Aloe Vera Gel</b> and <b>ground Snakegrass</b> once your <b>Herbology</b> is high enough.
