# Spear Fighting(Skill)  
> Shows how good you are at using spears. Increases your success chances when hunting with this weapon.   
> "This skill increases your <b>chances</b> of hunting animals when using a spear and lowers the chances that you'll get <b>hurt</b> in the process.<br><br>Spear hunting usually produces better results than other methods
